The Property

Property History

The Earl W. Jimerson Apartment is named in honor of the International President of the Amalgamated Meat Cutters and Butchers Workmen of North America, A.F.L. – C.I.O., as a testimonial to over forty years of self-sacrifice in behalf of labor and the general welfare of the country.
