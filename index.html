The Property

Property History

The Earl W. Jimerson Apartment is named in honor of the International President of the Amalgamated Meat Cutters and Butchers Workmen of North America, A.F.L. – C.I.O., as a testimonial to over forty years of self-sacrifice in behalf of labor and the general welfare of the country. His name symbolizes the highest and truest for of the spirit of Unionism.

Neighborhood

Brownsville, Brooklyn is a neighborhood full of vitality. Our diverse population enjoys great facilities, schools, transportation, entertainment and culture.

Map

The cooperative features robust door-to-door transportation systems as well as driving, walking and bicycling options. Get directions right to our front door. See transit maps, find nearby subway stations and bus stops that estimate travel time right from this website.

Live Here

Apartments

There is a reason our apartments are coveted by every New Yorker. Jimerson apartments are oversized and built with families in mind. 3 fifteen-story fireproof buildings containing 420 apartments each with their own balcony. The hallmarks of every Studio, 1, 2, and 3 bedroom apartment in the cooperative, are comfort and convenience. Each residence has a spacious foyer, ample closets, bright, sunny living rooms and bedrooms and terraces with spectacular views. Apartment breakdown are as follows; 84 Studio, 84 One Bedroom, 168 Two Bedrooms and 84 Three Bedrooms.

Amenities

Jimerson is a self-contained community which consists of 3 fifteen-story fireproof buildings containing 420 apartments each with their own balcony located on 197,230 square feet of land. The apartment houses will occupy only 15% of the land with the remaining grounds dedicated to landscaped gardens and walks with enclosed playgrounds for children. Our property features a landscaped plaza with tall shade trees, flowerbeds, sitting areas as well as 2 underground parking garages.

Application and Waiting List

Earl W. Jimerson Housing Corporation has waiting lists for all of its apartments. New applicants should use this site to obtain an application and to get on the waiting list. We look forward to welcoming you to one of Brooklyn’s most vibrant and exciting communities.

Safety

What We Do

The members of the Earl W. Jimerson Security Force are committed to delivering quality services in response to our community's diverse needs. We recognize our responsibility to protect life and property, prevent crime, enforce laws and improve the quality of life for all residents of the community. Additional Coverage is also provided by the 73rd Precinct of the NYPD.
